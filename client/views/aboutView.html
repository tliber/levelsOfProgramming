Github> The purpose of this project is to provide a roadmap of developers of all levels to help elucidate their individual strenghts and weaknesses. It was inspired by a blogpost by Sijin Joseph which helped me take my coding to new levels. As with his article, the levels and ideals of a complete fullstack developer are entirely subjective from my point of view. With that said, I aim to have the most accurate and complete presentation of the material ya programmer would need today to build out anything they might imagine
